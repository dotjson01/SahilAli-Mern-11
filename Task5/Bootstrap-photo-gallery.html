Popular Tourist Destinations in India

Taj Mahal, Agra

An ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna, symbol of eternal love.

Hawa Mahal, Jaipur

The Palace of Winds, a five-story palace in pink sandstone with intricate lattice work.

Ghats of Varanasi

Sacred riverfront steps leading to the holy Ganges River, spiritual heart of India.

Kerala Backwaters

Network of lagoons, lakes, and canals parallel to the Arabian Sea coast, perfect for houseboat cruises.

Golden Temple, Amritsar

The holiest Gurdwara of Sikhism, known for its golden architecture and spiritual significance.

Hampi, Karnataka

Ancient ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire, featuring stunning temple architecture and boulder landscapes.
